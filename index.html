I suspect that this is because I have really good garbage

I know this because it was the only thing in there.
I've taken to calling it "Garbage". It is black, fluffy, and cross-eyed.

Peanut Butter

I have a tremendously absurd relationship with peanut butter. I could eat it until the sheer quantity killed me.
I don't know why, but if there is an open jar in the house I can hear its siren-song constantly.
And, foolishly, I go. A sailor to the rocks.
Drowning before I'm even aware that the sweet sweet singing has stopped.

Stigmata

Brooke, my friend, tripped and fell into a holly bush and emerged with perfect stigmata markings.
Hands, feet, head, the whole ordeal. The very best part is that we didn't tell her deeply religious mother what happened when she came to pick her up.
I think we maybe caved and told her after about 30 minutes.
